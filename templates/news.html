Honda's First E-Bike for India to launch next year.

23 Nov 2023 4:30 P.M. IST

Image: HONDA SC e Concept

Honda plans to launch its first electric scooter in India next year, likely named Activa Electric. It will feature a fixed-type battery for affordability and is part of Honda's new modular EV architecture. The battery pack and components will be manufactured in India for cost competitiveness.

A second scooter with swappable batteries is also in the works, targeting a broader customer base.
TVS X launched in India
29 Oct 2023 7:70 P.M. IST

TVS has finally launched their new electric scooter X in India. The X is currently the most expensive electric scooter in India.!

TVS Motor Company recently rolled out the X, a new electric scooter at Rs 2.50 lakh (ex-showroom) in the Indian market. The TVS X will be the second electric scooter from the firm in India's lineup following the iQube. It is built on the recently created XLETON platform and incorporates a futuristic design approach.

The TVS X has three ride modes: Xtride, Xtealth, and Xonic, and uses an aluminum alloy frame that is reportedly 2.5 times stiffer than conventional scooter frames. It also has selectable regenerative braking and LED headlamps that resemble vertically stacked cubes. As per the two-wheeler manufacturer, the TVS X seeks to raise the bar for the electric scooter market in the country with its remarkable performance, cutting-edge technology, and distinctive look
