Honda's First E-Bike for India to launch next year.

23 Nov 2023 4:30 P.M. IST

Image: HONDA SC e Concept

Honda plans to launch its first electric scooter in India next year, likely named Activa Electric. It will feature a fixed-type battery for affordability and is part of Honda's new modular EV architecture. The battery pack and components will be manufactured in India for cost competitiveness.

A second scooter with swappable batteries is also in the works, targeting a broader customer base.
